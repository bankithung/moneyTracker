Privacy Policy

Your trust is our top priority.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Wealth Planner. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us to improve your financial planning experience:

  • Account Information: Phone number, name, and security PIN for authentication.
  • Financial Data: Income details, transaction records, savings goals, and budget preferences.
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns to improve service quality.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Providing and maintaining our service
  • Generating personalized financial insights
  • Improving our application and user experience
  • Ensuring the security of your account

4. Data Security

We implement bank-grade security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure PIN-based authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Data stored on secure, encrypted servers

5. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. To request account deletion, please visit our Account Deletion Request page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@wealthplanner.app.
